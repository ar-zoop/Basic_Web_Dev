Signature Black Forest Brownie

Recipe

Inredients

  1. Cherries ( 1 1/2 Cup )
  2. Powdered Sugar( 2 Tablespoons)
  3. Water ( 2 Tablespoons )
  4. Cornflour Mix ( 1 Tbl Spoon+ 1 Teaspoon + 1 Tbl Spoon Water )
  5. Dark And Milk Chocolate ( 1 Cup Each You Could Also Use One Kind Of Chocolate But It Should Be Total 2 Cups)
  6. Thick Cream ( 10 Tble Spoons )
  7. Thick Cream For Garnish

Photos from the recipe




What is so special about this brownie?

  1. Truffle sauce, Cherries, Whipped cream
  2. Fudgy, Gooey, Dense

Points to be noted

  1. Pre Bake My Signature Brownie Recipe
  2. The Jam Will Last For 2 Days In The Refrigerator
  3. Let The Jam Cool Completely Before Using

Extra Tips

  1. Let The Brownie Cool Completely Before Spreading The Jam
  2. The Brownies Will Last For 2 Days In The Refrigerator
  3. Serve With Any Kind Of Icecream

Photos from the recipe

What is so special about this pizza dough?

  1. No eggs
  2. No machine
  3. Soft and crsip at the same time

Points to be noted

  1. Do Not Mix In Really Hot Water It Should Be Lukewarm Such That You Could Touch It
  2. Cover And Place In Warm Area Not Really Hot Otherwise It Will Kill The Yeast

Extra Tips

  1. Preheat Your Oven For Atleast 20 Minutes (Pizza's S Like Really Hot Oven)
  2. Perfect Way To Check If It's Done Is To Just Lift The Pizza Gently From Its Side If It Is Golden Brown At The Bottom Then Its Done!!

Cinnamon Loaf

Recipe

video here

Ingredients

For the batter:
  1. Eggs(1)
  2. Brown sugar (1 Cup)
  3. Cinnamon( 1 Tsp)
  4. Baking soda( 1 Teaspoon )
  5. Butter(100 Grams)
  6. Buttermilk ( In One Cup Of Milk Squeeze A Complete Lemon And Ket It Rest For 12 Minutes)
For the cinnamon sugar:
  1. Brown sugar(6 Tbsp)
  2. Cinnamon powder (1 Tsp )
For the cinnamon cream:
  1. Cream cheese (2 Tbsp)
  2. Powdered sugar (2 Tbsp)
  3. Cream/Milk (2 Tblsp)
  4. Cinnamon powder ( 1 Tblsp)
  5. Vanilla extract ( 1 Drop)

Photos from the recipe

What is so special about this loaf?

  1. It's Super Moist
  2. Satisfaction In Every Bite

Points to be noted

  1. Do Not Over Mix The Batter
  2. Use ONLY Brown Sugar
  3. Store In An Airtight Container Do Not Refrigerate

Extra Tips

  1. Let It Rest For 15 Minutes After Baking Inside The Oven
  2. Serve With Fresh Brewed Coffee

My version of Dynamite Shrimp

Recipe

video here

Ingredients

  1. Mayonnaise( 2 Cups )
  2. Sriracha ( 1/2 Cup)
  3. Sweet Chilli Sauce( 1 Cup )
  4. Mayo Sriracha (Optional)(1/2 Cup )
  5. Soya Sauce ( 2 Tbl Spoons)
  6. Eggs( 5 )
  7. Flour ( 1cup ( As Per Your Quantity) )
  8. Cornstarch ( 2 Cups ( As Per Your Quantity) )
  9. You Can Adjust The Taste Of The Sauce According To Your Preference
For vegetarians:
  1. Replace The Shrimp For Paneer (Quantity As Desired)
  2. Replace The Eggs For ( 1 Cup Melted Butter)

Photos from the recipe

What is so special about this recipe?

  1. It's Homemade
  2. CRISPY AND TASTY

Points to be noted

  1. Clean Your Shrimps Properly
  2. Fry The Shrimps In Really Hot Oil
  3. Coat The Shrimps Properly In The Egg And Flour Mixture
  4. The Below Mentioned Ingredient Quantities Will Differ As Per How Much You Are Making

Extra Tips

  1. Coat The Hot And Crisp Shrimps Directly With The Sauce
  2. Repeat The Exact Same Process For Paneer

Rose Milk Cake

Recipe

video here

Ingredients

Cake Batter
  1. EGGS (6)
  2. FLOUR(1 Cup)
  3. BROWN SUGAR (1 Cup)
  4. CINNAMON,CARDAMOM( 1 Tsp)
  5. SAFFRON ( 4 Strands)
  6. MILK(2 Tbl Spoons)
  7. 2 DROPS VANILLA
Rose Syrup
  1. MILK(1/2 Cup)
  2. CONDENSED MILK(1/2 Cup)
  3. ROSE SYRUP ( 2 Tbsp )
  4. CARDAMOM (1 Tsp )

Photos from the recipe

What is so special about this Milk Cake?

  1. Soft And Spongy Cake Filled With Spices
  2. Infused Milk With Rose.

Points to be noted

  1. Do Not Deflate The Cake Batter
  2. Beat The Egg Whites Till They Double In Volume
  3. Make Sure You Sift The Flour

Extra Tips

  1. Make Sure Your Beaters Are Washed Properly No Amount Of Grease Should Be There On Them
  2. Serve The Dessert Cool
